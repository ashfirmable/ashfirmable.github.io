Analysis

Week in Review  ·  Week of 11 May 2026

The week of 11 May delivered 95 MQLs, a 4% easing from the prior week's 99 but 17% above the 4-week rolling average. Paid Search led with 54 leads (57% of total), followed by Organic Search (20) and Direct (19).

Opportunities fell to 35, down 17% week-on-week from 42 the prior week. Pipeline value came in at $27,226, down 55% from 4 May's $60,850 — however, that prior week was inflated by two large outlier deals: Crannull ($13,860, Direct) and Intelligencebank ($11,880, Organic Social). Stripping those out, the underlying 4 May pipeline was ~$35k, making the 11 May result a more moderate -22% decline on a like-for-like basis.

Closed Won generated $33,630 across 13 deals, up 12% in value from the prior week's $29,993. A caveat: the figure includes a single large win, Rohlig Logistics ($17,820, Other Source). Excluding that deal, CW value would have been $15,810. Deal count fell from 18 to 13.

Paid spend jumped to $6,274 (+47% WoW), alongside a traffic surge to 2,487 new users (+57%). Despite both metrics spiking, MQLs declined slightly, driving CPL up to $66 from $43 the prior week. The spend and traffic increase likely reflects a planned campaign push.

To watch this week: whether the normalisation in pipeline from 11 May reflects the absence of those two large 4 May outliers or signals a genuine slowdown in deal value. Also watch whether the elevated spend carries into the week of 18 May and drives pipeline recovery.
